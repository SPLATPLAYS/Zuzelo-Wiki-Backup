Community

Learn more about the amazing and diverse community of Zuzelo

Youtube

Zuzelo's youtube channel is the foundation of the ZuzeloApps project, it totalize's 26,000 members and 157 videos! The youtube community has a pretty strong base thanks to many Youtube Lives (Accompanied by Twitch streams), that started in june 2024 and brought a lot of exchange among the members of the community and with zuzelo himself.

Discord

Zuzelo's Discord is one of if not the most active part of his community, totalling around 600 members at it's peak; it allows people to discuss, share their art and participate in contest's made by zuzelo.

Discord Contest's are usually just art contest's but 4 have impacted zuzelo's channel a bit more than others.

Patreon

Zuzelo's Patreon is mainly how the ZuzeloApps project gets funded, in the Patreon you can find various extra content, early release of videos, games, and simulations that Zuzelo made and much more. Moreover, Patreon subscribers get a special rank in the Siscord and some special thanks in each video!

Twitch

Zuzelo's Twitch Channel, made in july 2024 allowed people to talk with Zuzelo and learn some game dev and ai tips. Most of these streams are to chill and discussion based but sometimes streams are used by zuzelo to get inspiration on a project! With Zuzelo being a multi platform streamer he streams to both Twitch and Youtube.
