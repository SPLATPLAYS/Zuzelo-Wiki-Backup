Eras

Since it's beginning Penguin's Dungeon has passed many different periods, characterized by different users and activity

The Beginning Era

Not much is known since only a few people were active during this time. Zuzelo, the owner, Radnnugget, and Boris Borisov as well as Lasse were the only active, though this activity was very little.

The Structure Era

The server started to take shape but with it also came some problematic personnalities. To solve those problems Zuzelo named Radnugget mod. Very little discussion was happening and there were only 3 or 4 active persons. This era was before Zuzelo's channel hit 10 000 subscribers and the server was only at it's beginning.

The Golden Trio Era

To celebrate (and just before) hitting 10 000 subscribers, Zuzelo did a promotion of the discord server in one of his videos, giving a special role (From Ze Start) to anyone here before 10K subs. This brought a lot of members to the server. However Zuzelo, Radnugget, and Boris Borisov talked way less than in the beginning Era and there were only about 6 or 7 active members that talked and participated in contest. Almost all of the discussions were between the Golden Trio of Lasse, Slipte, and Seldon, a new member.

Its important to note that SPLATPLAYS joined in this era but was barely active for the coming months till the begginings of the Stream Era.

The dark Era

At some point discussion started to reduce and contest were less frequent, at this time, HoneyBadger made his appearance in the server, the server was less and less active every day besides some events like the glorious beer chain by Mantvyy and the creation of the "beertender" role. (The role is for making a small but substancial donation to Zuzelo, SPLATPLAYS later gained this role in the Stream Era)

The Stream Era

Zuzelo decided to start streaming which brought a fresh air to all the community, thanks to promotion by discord members like SPLATPLAYS, Seldon, and even Zuzelo. From the streams many people started joining or became active such as GentleEgo, Prepriegrane, Andasen, SPLATPLAYS, Marshtoast and others, this growth also gave birth to another server, THE CAUSE, where active users were invited to discuss and plan things without Zuzelo. (See THE CAUSE) (THE CAUSE was brought about by community members anger towards Zuzelo harming Children that he clearly knows don't have what he wants) The server was more active than ever with fun interaction and games between Zuzelo and the community primarily on streams and less so on the discord server, much more fanart was created, and a new channel for programming discussion was created. There were also much more fun events such as The Cause Uprising, the GNTH stream series or Seldon's and MarshToasts wedding (??? Wrong place for this).

The Stagnant Era

As the novelty of streams began to fade and Zuzelo began to have some difficulty with his editing style the activity came down, while maintaining some more discussion then The Dark Era and The Golden Trio Era.

The Current Era

With the return of Zuzelo and some other member of the community some new projects begin to be put in place such as this Wiki (Developed by SPLATPLAYS and maintained by the community) or The soon to come return of streams, the server is getting more active then before and it is a good omen for the future

Contest

Discord Contest are usually just art contest but 4 have impacted zuzelo's channel a bit more than others

- An art contest was made to make the banner of the Youtube Channel

- A contest was made to decide the introduction of a new unit in the famous Ai Wars Series . That's how the Frenchman Unit got introduced in Ai Wars, as an idea proposed by discord user Lassé, one of the most active members of the discord at the time.

- Finally, two contest were made more recently to choose a idea for a new video.

The first contest resulted in the "Ai Beats Tower Defense" video which was extremely popular; proposed by user Seldon08 also one of the most active members of the discord.

In the second contest user SPLATPLAYS , founder of the wiki, and major helper in the community, won by proposing the idea "Ai learns to conquest planets" which would then be changed with some added suggestions from other members to "Ai learns to survive in Space" which hasn't come out yet at this time!
