AI Wars Units

Explore the powerful units available in AI Wars, their roles, and abilities.

Melee Units

Warrior

Role: Heavy melee fighter

Abilities: High health and powerful close-range attacks.

Description: Equipped with a shield and sword, the Warrior excels in close combat, absorbing damage while dealing heavy blows.

Knight

Role: Fast and powerful melee flanker

Abilities: Super fast movement, pierce, needs space.

Description: With his horse and his spear, The Knight rolls around the battlefield, piercing through enemies line.

Undead

Role: Swarm Enemy

Abilities: One Shot Melee Attack, Conversion

Description: Despite being slow and weak the Undead can one shot all units, turning them into undead and growing their army, they are swarming enemy lines and when they get to you, it's too late.

Ranged Units

Musketeer

Role: Long-range marksman

Abilities: Precision shooting with high single-target damage.

Description: Armed with a musket, the Musketeer is effective at picking off enemies from a distance but is vulnerable in melee combat.

Support Units

Healer

Role: Team sustainer

Abilities: Restores health to allies and provides buffs.

Description: Essential for keeping the team alive during extended battles. Weak in direct combat.
