AI Wars Units

Melee Units

Warrior

Role: Heavy melee fighter

Abilities: High health and powerful close-range attacks.

Description: Equipped with a shield and sword, the Gladiator excels in close combat, absorbing damage while dealing heavy blows.

Ranged Units

Musketeer

Role: Long-range marksman

Abilities: Precision shooting with high single-target damage.

Description: Armed with a musket, the Musketeer is effective at picking off enemies from a distance but is vulnerable in melee combat.

Support Units

Healer

Role: Team sustainer

Abilities: Restores health to allies and provides buffs.

Description: Essential for keeping the team alive during extended battles. Weak in direct combat.

Special Units
