Introduction

Role: Versatile Opponent

Abilities: Can heal itself by reducing his melee attack and great area damage

Description: Equipped with a baguette and a croissant, the Frenchman is effective against big groups and can withstand certain hits with his baguette and deal decent melee damage with his baguette.

Weakness: Very weak if friendly fire is turned on, usually groups kill themselves if friendly fire is activated,

Variations

OG Frenchman

  • Can move freely
  • Can rotate freely
  • 100 health
  • 2 move speed
  • 50 base damage
  • Attack with their baguette for melee damage or their croissant for ranged area damage, can heal itself by sacrificing part of their melee damage

Trivia

The Frenchman is the 7th unit added to AI Wars.

The Frenchman was proposed by Lasse, an active Discord member, and voted 19 times, almost twice the average of other propositions.

Instead of fighting a "red" team as usual, Frenchmen fought against "humanity" because French are obviously eldritch horror-level threats.
