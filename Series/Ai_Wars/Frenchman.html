Frenchman

A Versatile Opponent in AI Wars

Role Versatile Opponent
Abilities Can heal itself by reducing melee attack; strong area damage
Description Equipped with a baguette and a croissant; effective against groups; can withstand hits and deal melee damage.
Weakness Very weak with friendly fire on; tends to cause team self-destruction.

Introduction

The Frenchman is a humorous but effective unit introduced in AI Wars. Designed as a nod to classic French stereotypes, this unit blends comedic flavor with real tactical advantage.

Variations

Og Frenchman

  • Can move freely
  • Can rotate freely
  • 100 health
  • 2 move speed
  • 50 base damage
  • Attacks with a baguette (melee) or croissant (ranged AoE)
  • Can heal by sacrificing melee power

Trivia

The Frenchman is the 7th unit added to AI Wars.

Proposed by Lasse, an active Discord member, and received 19 votes—nearly double the average.

Unlike other units, the Frenchman battles “humanity” instead of the red team, emphasizing their mythic, chaotic nature.
