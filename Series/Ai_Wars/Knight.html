Introduction

Role: Basic Melee Warrior

Abilities: Strong Melee Attack and Block

Description: The Warrior is the most basic unit of Ai Wars it does good melee damage and can block attacks but that's about it

Weakness: No special capability , super weak to zombies , weak to ranged attacks , weak to fast soldiers

 

Variations

OG Knight:

  • can move freely
  • can sprint (cost 10 stamina per second)
  • can rotate freely
  • can dodge (cost 25 stamina)
  • 100 Health
  • 100 Stamina
  • 2 move speed
  • 4 sprint speed
  • 100 ATK damage
  • 1 ATK speed (1 per second)
  • Attack by swinging his sword (cost 25 stamina)

 

Trivia

The Warrior is the first unit that was added to the AI_Wars series.

The Warrior is the unit that got the most variations

He's one of the most shown units, being included in 8 out of 13 AI Wars videos!
