Introduction

Role: Fast and powerful melee flanker

Abilities: Super fast movement, pierce, needs space.

Description: With his horse and his spear, The Knight rolls around the battlefield, piercing through enemies line.

Weakness: Weak in tight places, doesn't have a lot of hp

 

Variations

OG Knight:

  • Can only charge forward
  • Can rotate freely
  • Can pierce
  • Attack's by just going through enemies.
  • 50 Health
  • 8 speed
  • 20 Max speed
  • 100 ATK damage
  • When charging the knight's move speed keep increasing toward the max speed, the speed reset when the charge is interrupted.

 

Trivia

The Knight is the fifth unit that was added to the AI_Wars series.
