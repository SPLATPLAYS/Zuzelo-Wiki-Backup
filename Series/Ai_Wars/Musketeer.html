Introduction

Role: Long-range marksman

Abilities: Precision shooting with high single-target damage.

Description: Armed with a musket, the Musketeer is effective at picking off enemies from a distance but is vulnerable in melee combat.

Weakness: Weak at short range, no defence capabilities, no area damage, friendly fire

 

Variations

OG musketeer:

  • can move freely
  • can rotate freely ( 50 rotation speed)
  • 100 Health
  • 2 move speed
  • 50 ATK damage
  • 0.33 ATK speed
  • Attack by shooting with the musket

Improved musketeer:

  • has more friendly fire punishment
  • has better aim
  • More existence punishment (as always)

    • Frenchman era musketeer:

    • damage lowered from 50 to 25
    • attack speed lowered from 0.5/s to 0.25/s
    • Overall, twice as weak as the improved musketeer
