Basic Tower

This tower appears in the first video of the serie and is similar to an Archer Tower in most games This tower has 2 tiers: At tier 1: - 2 tiles of range - 5 attack damage - 0.5 attack per second (1 every 2s) At tier 2: - 4 tile of range - 10 attack damage - 1 attack per second (1 every second)
