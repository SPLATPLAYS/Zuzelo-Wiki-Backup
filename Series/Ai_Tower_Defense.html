Ai Tower Defense

An amazing serie based on the Tower Defense genre where an ai has to manage different strategies to beat various ennemies

Towers

Basic Tower

This tower appears in the first video of the serie and is similar to an Archer Tower in most games

This tower has 2 tiers:

At tier 1:

- 2 tiles of range

- 5 attack damage

- 0.5 attack per second (1 every 2s)

At tier 2:

- 4 tile of range

- 10 attack damage

- 1 attack per second (1 every second)

- Can attack any type of Unit

Ranger Tower

This tower is the first presented in the second video of the serie and is similar to the Basic Tower of episode 1

This tower has:

- 50 attack damage (ennemies and towers were buff in episode 2 , it's not like it's 10x better than the Basic Tower)

- 1 attack per second (1 every second)

- Can attack any type of units

Ice Tower

This tower is the second tower presented in the second video of the serie and is more of a support tower

This tower has:

- 0 attack damage (since it's a support it can't inflict damage)

- 3 attack per second (3 every second)

- Can attack any type of units

- This towers attack reduce the ennemie's speed by half (cannot stack)

- Unlike other tower it doesn't always target the first ennemie, it will target the first one without the freeze effect

Cannon Tower

This tower is the third tower presented in the second video of the serie and is an big aoe damage dealer

This tower has:

- 75 attack damage (since it's a support it can't inflict damage)

- 0.33 attack per second (1 every 3 seconds)

- Can't attack airborn ennemies
